## NEOTNY Circuit


To program the NEOTNY, use the Debug header, and connect it with the Arduino to programm it in ISP mode.

Debug Header Pins	-	Arduino Pins
1: VCC	-	5V
2: GND	-	GND
3: RESET	-	10 (used as RESET)
4: MOSI	-	11 (MOSI)
5: MISO	-	12 (MISO)
6: SCLK	-	13 (SCLK)

…don’t forget the 10uF Elko!


Buttons: 
- Left: PB2
- Right: PA7

- Up: PA2
- Down: PA1

Display:
- SCL: SCK / PA4
- SDA: MOSI / PA6
